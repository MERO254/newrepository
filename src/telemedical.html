Theracare Telemedicine Platform

I participated in a hackathon where our team developed a comprehensive telemedical app and website named Theracare. This platform bridges the gap between patients and healthcare providers, offering seamless virtual consultations and medical services.

Theracare was designed with the vision of making healthcare accessible to everyone, regardless of geographical location. It enables patients to consult with specialists from around the world, particularly valuable for managing chronic conditions and during health crises like the COVID-19 pandemic.

My Role

As the Mobile App Developer and Team Leader, I spearheaded the development process, coordinated team efforts, and ensured seamless integration of all platform components. My responsibilities included architecting the app's structure, implementing key features, and maintaining code quality throughout the project lifecycle.

Key Features

  • Secure user authentication with email verification
  • Doctor discovery and specialization filtering
  • Real-time appointment scheduling system
  • Integrated chat functionality with end-to-end encryption
  • Digital prescription generation and sharing
  • AUTODOC - AI medical assistant powered by Gemini AI
  • Comprehensive user and doctor profiles
  • Availability management for healthcare providers
  • Medical history tracking and updates
  • Cross-platform compatibility
